ABOUT MET

Researcher Group

A research team is a group of people who are committed to achieving a common objective.
The team's common objective in this case is the successful research, data analysis, publication, and dissemination of important findings.
In a successful research team, each member has a distinct role and collaborates closely.
Each team member needs to understand their function and how it relates to the overall project.

Social Administrator Group

Using a unified voice, a social media administrator is in charge of managing interactions between the campaign and the public on social media platforms.
They create and implement content strategies, gather engagement information, etc.
In order to help develop digital initiatives that foster brand loyalty, social media administrators also discover tendencies among respondents' interactions.

Designer Group

A designing team consists of the individuals, the roles they play, the goals of the team members, the tools and techniques they employ, and the project structures within which they work.
They are in charge of coming up with and capturing ideas for the functionality, appearance, and behavior of the product.
There are designer specialties in many fields of design.

Programmer Group

Coding or computer programmers, are responsible for designing, coding, testing, modifying, and implementing new or existing software products.
Their duties will include liaising with the development managers, writing clean, scalable code, creating testing protocols, fixing bugs, and deploying programs.
They should possess in-depth knowledge of programming languages, superior problem-solving abilities, and the capacity to meet deadlines if they hope to succeed as a developer.
